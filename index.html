Dry Van

Is an enclosed truck trailer with a solid roof and sides. It's used for transporting general cargo that doesn't require temperature control. Think of it as a large, weatherproof box on wheels.

Reefer

is designed to trasport temperature-sensitive cargo. It has a refrigeration unit connected to the power supply onboard. Reefer containers maintain specific temperatures for goods

Flat Bed/Step Deck

has an open, flat platform without sides or a roof. It's ideal for carrying oversized or irregularly shaped cargo, such as construction materials,machinery or largde pipes

Power Only

Refers to a service where third-party carriers provide the truck (the "power") while another company supplies the trailers

Box Truck

have a fully enclosed cargo area. They're versatile and commonly used for local deliveries, moving services, or transporting smaller loads

Hot Shot

involves hauling time-sensitive or high-priority cargo. It's often done with smaller trucks or vans. Hots shots are used for urgent deliveries, expedited freight, or specailized loads
